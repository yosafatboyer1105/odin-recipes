The Recipe for Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delectable Italian dish renowned for its harmonious blend of flavors and layers. Comprising sheets of wide pasta, lasagna is meticulously assembled with alternating strata of rich, savory meat sauce, velvety béchamel sauce, and a generous sprinkling of melted cheese.

The amalgamation of these components creates a savory and comforting experience. Baked to perfection, the lasagna emerges from the oven with golden-brown edges, enticing both the eyes and the palate. This beloved dish is a testament to the culinary finesse of Italian cuisine, making it a favorite on tables worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 9 sheets of lasagna noodles, boiled according to package instructions
  • 1 pound (about 500 grams) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (about 800 grams) crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cups (about 500 grams) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups (about 250 grams) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (about 250 grams) grated mozzarella cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat a little oil. Sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce, and stir. Add sugar, salt, black pepper, oregano, and basil. Bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg, stirring until well combined.
  6. Take a lasagna pan and spread a bit of tomato sauce on the bottom.
  7. Arrange 3 sheets of lasagna noodles over the sauce, then add half of the tomato sauce, half of the ricotta mixture, and sprinkle some Parmesan and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat this process to create the second layer.
  9. Top with the final layer of lasagna noodles, remaining tomato sauce, and a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the filling is cooked through.
  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade Lasagna!
